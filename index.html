About ToolsLab

Your trusted free digital toolkit — built for every Indian citizen by Digital Hub

Welcome to ToolsLab, a free and comprehensive online platform developed and maintained by Digital Hub. Our mission is simple: to make everyday digital tasks faster, easier, and more accessible for every person in India — whether you are a student preparing a resume for your first job interview, a small business owner who needs a quick professional poster, a shopkeeper who wants to print an ID card layout, or a citizen who needs to reach a government portal quickly.

In today's fast-moving digital world, people often struggle to find the right tools — they either require expensive software, complicated installations, or force you to upload sensitive personal documents to unknown servers. ToolsLab solves all of these problems. Every tool on our platform runs entirely within your web browser using modern JavaScript technology. This means your files, photos, and personal information never leave your device. There is no registration required, no subscription fee for core tools, and no data collection.

Our Story

ToolsLab was founded by the team at Digital Hub after realizing that millions of Indians, especially in tier-2 and tier-3 cities, lacked access to simple, reliable digital tools. Common service center tasks — such as printing Aadhaar card layouts, creating passport-size photos, converting images to PDF, or building a quick resume — either required expensive software or meant visiting a cyber café. We decided to change that by building a single, powerful, free platform accessible from any device with a browser — including smartphones.

Since our launch, ToolsLab has grown to offer 60+ tools across five major categories, along with direct links to over 35 official Indian government portals. We continuously expand our tool library based on real feedback from our users across India.

What We Offer

ToolsLab is divided into five powerful categories, each designed to serve a specific set of everyday digital needs:

  • Essential & Services: Resume/CV builder, passport photo layout, photo resize and crop, payment QR code generator, mock tests, and more.
  • Creative Design Studio: Pro resume maker, marriage biodata creator, professional ID card designer, poster and banner maker, application letter generator, and shop promotion designer.
  • Smart PDF & Image Tools: Convert JPG/PNG images to PDF, merge multiple PDFs into one, delete individual pages from a PDF, and convert PNG images to JPEG — all in your browser without any upload.
  • Card Printing Services: Print-ready card layout templates for Aadhaar, Voter ID, Driving Licence, Health cards, Tax ID, School/College ID, and Food Subsidy cards.
  • Government Portal Links: 35+ curated direct links to official Indian government websites including UIDAI, NVSP, EPFO, IRCTC, PMJAY, PM Kisan, Passport Seva, GST, and many more.
